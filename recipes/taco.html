Taco

A well made taco, makes your stomach happy.
This is a family secret wich I will now share with the world.


Ingridients

  • Taco Shell
  • Paprika
  • Sour Cream
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla Chips

Steps

  1. Warm the shells up
  2. Cut up the paprika
  3. Add sour cream into the shell
  4. Chop up the meat and stake it
  5. Put cheese in the shell
  6. Have tortilla chips on the side
